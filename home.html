Welcome to the Sussex College Network

The idea of Sussex as an affordable fee levying private school network is to teach the government syllabus in English Medium preserving the cultural and traditional values. Sussex College Network also pride ourselves by giving students possibilities like never before with personalized learning, new course offerings, and passion pathways. Students at our colleges find their true selves rather than letting a system define who they can become or how far they can go. As a school we aim to nurture every student so that they can be the best they can be; global citizens with a sense of adventure,who are able to discover where their skills and passions lie, and harness these to inform future careers.
